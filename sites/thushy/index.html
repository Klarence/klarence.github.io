Originally Thushy tried to get Klarence to move to Boston. But it wasn't in the cards. It's looks like we only move West. Next stop San Francisco, or maybe... Hawaii?

First Vacation

We had several trip together because we were in a long distance relationship. We knew we enjoyed travelling together. We went on our first vacation within three-months of meeting each other. We went to Puerto Rico with a few of Thushy's friends.

Thushy moved to San Jose

A long distance relationship was really hard on Thushy. She needed to know if we were only enjoying the relationship because we were in vacay mode when we met with each other. It was a tough decision, but one that we were both happy with.
